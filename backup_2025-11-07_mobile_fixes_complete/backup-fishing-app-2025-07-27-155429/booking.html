Bignor Park Carp Fishery Rules

The Following Rules will apply from 1st May 2025

General Rules

  • All Vehicles must display their Membership Cards whilst on the Estate.
  • All Vehicles must be parked by the lake entrance and not outside the Estate cottages or Shooting Hut.
  • When Driving on the Estate please do not exceed 20mph.
  • All Gates must be kept closed and vehicle access kept clear.
  • Each Member must be in possession of a Valid Rod License.
  • Members must Attend at least 1 work party per year.

Fishing Rules

  • 3 Rods Maximum.
  • 3 Anglers Maximum.
  • Micro Barbed hooks only.
  • No Rods left unattended at any time.
  • Minimum 15lb Mainline no Braid except Marker rods.
  • No Naked Mainline please use 24inch min tubing/Leaders.
  • No Fixed Leads if using lead clips please do not jam the tale rubber Tail all the way on.
  • When fishing to Snags, Lily Pads or Thick weed please fish locked up.
  • Minimum 42" Landing Net Required.
  • Large Unhooking Mat and Floatation Weigh Sling Required.

Care & Safety

  • Please Dip Mats, Slings and Nets in BioVx Bin at gate before you fish.
  • Please only retain carp for short period of time while you weigh and photograph them.
  • All hook holds must be treated using Propolis.
  • All captures & weights MUST be reported to Michael
  • Any Catfish caught must be retained a sling is provided in the green box by the gate

Estate Rules

  • No Block Bookings (book one session at a time)
  • 24Hr or Overnight Fishing Must be Booked with Michael.
  • 24Hr Means from Midday to Midday.
  • No Loud Music or Shouting across the lake.
  • No Drugs or excessive Alcohol consumption.
  • No Open Fires this is an Estate rule.
  • All Litter must be taken with you.
  • No Swimming in the Lake under any circumstances.
  • The use of Waders are at one's own risk.

Your Membership will be at risk if any of the above rules are broken.

Any Flouting of these rules will result in immediate termination of Membership.
