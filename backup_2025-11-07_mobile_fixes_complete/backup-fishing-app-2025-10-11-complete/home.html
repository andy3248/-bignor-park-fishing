Bignor Main Lake

Our flagship venue offers spacious, pristine waters home to some truly magnificent carp. With a maximum capacity of just 3 anglers, you'll enjoy peaceful, uncrowded sessions.

The lake features commons, mirrors, and leather carp ranging from doubles up to specimens over 40lbs.

Fishing Rules & Regulations

Estate & Vehicle Rules

  • Display membership cards on estate
  • Maximum 20mph speed limit
  • Park by lake entrance only

Equipment

  • 3 rods maximum
  • 3 anglers maximum
  • Micro barbed hooks only
  • Minimum 15lb mainline

Fish Care

  • Dip mats in BioVx bin before fishing
  • Treat hook holds with Propolis
  • Report all captures to Michael
